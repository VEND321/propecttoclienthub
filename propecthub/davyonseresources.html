About Us

At Davyonse Resources, we are committed to delivering the highest quality agricultural and semi-agricultural produce to meet the needs of our growing communities. With a focus on sustainable farming practices, we cultivate a diverse range of crops that are both nutritious and environmentally friendly.

Our dedicated team combines traditional farming expertise with modern techniques to ensure consistency, freshness, and excellence in every harvest. Whether it’s fresh produce straight from our fields or semi-processed agricultural products ready for market, Davyonse Resources is your trusted partner in fostering a healthier, more sustainable future.

Our Products

Our agricultural and semi-agricultural products are designed to support sustainable farming practices while delivering high-quality, natural solutions to meet the growing demand for eco-friendly and efficient agricultural practices. These products are sourced from carefully selected farms and processed to maintain their integrity and nutritional value. From fresh, farm-to-table produce to semi-processed goods such as dried fruits, seeds, and grains, we ensure that every product meets the highest standards of quality and sustainability

OFADA RICE

Indulge in the authentic taste of locally sourced Ofada rice! Packed with rich, earthy flavors and unmatched aroma, our premium Ofada rice is perfect for your favorite traditional dishes. Nutritious, wholesome, and 100% natural, it’s a must-have for every kitchen.

IJEBU GARRI {5KG}

Experience the crunch and taste of tradition with our premium Ijebu Garri! Perfectly processed for that distinct sour flavor and crisp texture, it's ideal for drinking or making delicious eba. Rich, refreshing, and satisfying—Ijebu Garri is your go-to for authentic enjoyment.

HARVESTED CASSAVA TUBER

Our cassava is rich in carbohydrates, making it an excellent energy source for various food and industrial applications. With a commitment to excellence, we deliver farm-fresh tubers that meet the highest standards, providing a reliable ingredient for flour, starch, and other cassava-based products.

PLANTAIN FLOUR

Discover the natural goodness of our premium plantain flour! Gluten-free, nutritious, and packed with essential vitamins, it’s perfect for making swallow, pancakes, or smoothies. Enjoy its rich taste and health benefits in every bite.

HARVESTED PLANTAIN

Enjoy the rich taste and versatility of our fresh, organic plantains! Perfect for frying, boiling, or roasting, they’re packed with nutrients and full of natural flavor. every meal a delight with the best quality plantains.
